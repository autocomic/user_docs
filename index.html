Get started#

Each section of this manual describes how to use each feature of the Autocomic system.

Start by importing your favourite manga and jump into our editor. You can:

  1. Clean speech bubbles.
  2. Translate text in the speech bubbles.
  3. Inpaint - remove text outside of speech bubbles from the background.
